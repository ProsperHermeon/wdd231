Why Join?

The Prosper Chamber of Commerce provides networking, marketing, training, and advocacy to help local businesses thrive. Choose the membership level that fits your goals and start connecting with the Prosper business community today.

NP Membership Benefits

  • Standard directory listing
  • Access to community networking events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Eligible for community service awards

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Everything in NP, plus:
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo
  • Invitations to quarterly luncheons
  • 10% discount on event booth registration
  • Social media mention upon joining

Silver Membership Benefits

  • Everything in Bronze, plus:
  • Featured directory listing
  • 2 free training workshop seats per year
  • 25% discount on advertising packages
  • Eligible for home-page business spotlight
  • Priority event registration

Gold Membership Benefits

  • Everything in Silver, plus:
  • Premium spotlight placement on home page
  • Unlimited training workshop seats
  • Free annual event sponsorship slot
  • Exclusive executive networking dinners
  • Full-page newsletter feature once per year
  • Dedicated social media promotion monthly
